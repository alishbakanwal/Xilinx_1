Physical Constraints Window

The Vivado Design Suite supports floorplanning complex designs to break them into smaller modules of related functionality, and to preserve or assign areas of the device to specific regions, or Pblocks of the design. Floorplanning can help a design meet timing, and should be used when a design has difficulty meeting timing.

The Physical Constraints window displays the currently defined Pblocks in the design, and lets you manage those floorplanning regions. Pblocks can be defined in the Vivado IDE using the Tools > Floorplanning menu items.

The window has a toolbar that incliudes the following commands:

Search Opens a search field to enter a search pattern.
Expand all Expands the Pblock hierarchy to show all of the area regions defined in the design.
Collapse all Collapses the Pblock hierarchy to show only the ROOT, or top-level region.
Scroll Scrolls the display to show the currently selected object.
