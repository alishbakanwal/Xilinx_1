Partial Reconfiguration Summary

This page summarizes the details of the Partial Reconfiguration wizard based on your selections. Details of the Reconfigurable Modules (RM), PR Configurations, and Configuration Runs are displayed. In addition, information is provided on RMs that are not used in the defined configurations. Look over the summary to be sure your selections are correct.

  • Click Back to return to prior pages to change selections as needed.
  • Click Finish to implement your choices as indicated.
  • Click Cancel to close the wizard without setting up Partial Reconfiguration.
