Clock Regions Window

The Clock Regions window lists the defined clock regions for the target part of the current project, shows their XY grid location, and lists any I/O Banks that are found in the clock region. Selecting a clock region in the window also selects that clock region in other windows, such as the Device and Package windows. The properties of a selected clock region are displayed in the Properties window.
