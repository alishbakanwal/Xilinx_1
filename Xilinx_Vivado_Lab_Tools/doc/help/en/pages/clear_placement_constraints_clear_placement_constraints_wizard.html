Clear Placement Constraints Wizard

The Clear Placement Constraints wizard lets you choose to unplace previously placed cells or I/O ports, or both, from the current design. The process that the Clear Placement Constraints wizard follows depends upon the state of the design, and choices that you make as you move through the wizard. Are there fixed and unfixed cells or ports in the design? Have you selected ports or cells prior to launching the wizard?

  • What type of placement do you want to clear?
    • Cell placement: Clear the placement constraints for the leaf, or primitive cells in the current design.
    • I/O port placement: Clear the I/O port assignments for ports in the design.
    • Both: Clear both the cell placements and port assignments in the current design.

After selecting to clear placement for cells, ports, or both, select Next to proceed. The page that is opened next is determined by your choice, and what objects you may have selected prior to running Clear Placement. The pages include:

  • Unplace Ports
  • Unplace Cells
  • Cell Types to Unplace
  • Fixed Placement
