Elaborated Design

The Elaborated Design environment encapsulates the in-memory design view of the various design sources (RTL, DCP, EDF), design constraints (XDC), and the target device or board. RTL elaboration of the top-level design runs RTL linting checks, infers logic from the RTL and builds design data structures, and optionally applies design constraints.

Opening the elaborated design is the quickest way to check for syntax errors in your RTL source files. During elaboration, the tool is looking at basic syntax, and verifying there are no syntax errors, evaluating parameters or generics, setting port sizes, and checking global connectivity.

When an Elaborated Design is open, you can activate the Elaborated Design environment by clicking RTL Analysis in the Flow Navigator.

From the Elaborated Design environment you can:

  • View the hierarchy of the design.
  • Open a schematic view of the elaborated netlist to better understand how the Vivado tool is interpreting the RTL source files.
  • Perform early I/O and clock planning and placement.
  • Run DRCs and report switching noise on the ports.
  • Perform behavioral simulation of the design.
