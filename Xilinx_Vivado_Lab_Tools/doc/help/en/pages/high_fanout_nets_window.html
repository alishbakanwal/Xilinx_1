High Fanout Nets

The High Fanout Nets window displays the results of the Report High Fanout Nets command. The window is divided into two sections. The left side has a table of contents, including General Information and Summary. The right side displays a list of the high fanout nets, with fanout numbers and driver type.

The High Fanout Nets toolbar includes the following commands:

Search Opens a search field to enter a search pattern.
Collapse all Collapses the left side hierarchy of the High Fanout Nets window.
Expand all Expands the left side hierarchy of the High Fanout Nets window.
Previous Restore the previous view of the High Fanout Nets window.
Next Return from the previous view.
Tip: This command is only available after using the Previous command.
Rerun Rerun the Report High Fanout Nets command.
