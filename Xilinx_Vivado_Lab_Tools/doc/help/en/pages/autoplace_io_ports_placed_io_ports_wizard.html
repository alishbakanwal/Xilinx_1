Placed I/O Ports

The page is only displayed when there are previously placed ports in the design. In this case the wizard reports the number of placed ports in the design, or the number that are currently selected, and asks how you would like to proceed:

  • Keep the <#> placed ports in their current location: Do not unplace and replace, or otherwise move, the already placed ports.
  • Find new locations for these <#> ports: unplace and replace the ports.
    Tip: Based on design connections and constraints, the autoplacer may choose to reassign a port to its current IOB and package pin.
