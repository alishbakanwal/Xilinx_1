Reports Window

In Project Mode, report files are automatically generated as part of the Vivado® synthesis, implementation, and bitstream generation processes. The Reports window lists the report files that are generated by the Vivado Design Suite as it runs through the design flow.

The report hierarchy is populated with placeholders even though some steps in the process have not been run. The reports are sorted according to the design process that generates them. Placeholder reports that are not generated will display as grey in the Reports window.

You can Collapse All () the folders down to Synthesis and Implementation folders, or Expand All () the folders to show the reports.

Double-click a report, or use the Open command in the right-click menu to open the file in the Text Editor.

Use the Search toolbar button () to search for reports by report title.
