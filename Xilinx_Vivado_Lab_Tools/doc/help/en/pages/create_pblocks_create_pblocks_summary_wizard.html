Create Pblocks Summary

This page summarizes the details of the Pblocks that will be created based on your selections. Look over the summary to be sure your selections are correct.

  • Click Back to return to prior pages to change selections as needed.
  • Click Finish to create the Pblocks as indicated.
  • Click Cancel to close the wizard without creating new Pblocks.

After creating and assigning cells to the Pblock, you will need to define the Pblock as a region in the Device window using the Draw Pblock command. You can assign additional cells to a Pblock after it has been created.
