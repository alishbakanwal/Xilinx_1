Synthesized Design

The Synthesized Design environment links post-synthesis netlist files and design constraints with the device resources of the target part to create the in-memory synthesized design.

When a synthesized design is open, you can activate the Synthesized Design environment by clicking Synthesis in the Flow Navigator.

From the Synthesized Design environment you can:

  • View the hierarchy of the design.
  • Open a schematic view of the synthesized netlist.
  • Perform I/O and clock planning and placement.
  • Start debug of the hardware design by inserting debug cores and hardware probes into the synthesized netlist.
  • Perform floorplanning of the design to manually place elements of the design or hierarchy.
  • Generate multiple reports, including timing and power analysis.
  • Perform functional and timing simulation with delay estimates of the synthesized design.
  • Launch implementation to place and route the design in memory.
